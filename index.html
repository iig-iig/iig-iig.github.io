About Our Chef

With over 15 years of culinary experience trained in Paris and Australia, Chef Jay brings a unique fusion of French techniques and Sri Lankan and European flavors to create unforgettable dining experiences.

His philosophy centers on using the freshest seasonal ingredients to create dishes that are both visually stunning and exceptionally delicious.

After working at several Jayin-starred restaurants across Europe and Asia, Chef Jay decided to bring his expertise to create a dining experience that combines sophistication with approachable flavors.
